Formless Minions

zzzzzZZZZZzzzz

Strange Creatures, they seem to buzz.

Stats

HP: 3,000
DEF: 15

Description

A curious creature, the seem powerless without one to protect. They will charge toward their master, and, upon receiving commands, will transform into a for in which they will be able to best complete their commands.

Shots

Projectile Damage Effect Range Speed Comments
100 - 5 Tiles 1.5 Tile/sec Formless Bolts
0 Quiet for 1 second 5 Tiles 1 Tile/sec Formless Yellow Discharge
0 Blind for 5 seconds 5 Tiles 1 Tile/sec Formless Blue Discharge
Slowed for 2 seconds - 5 Tiles 1 Tile/sec Formless Green Discharge

Phases

The minions charge towards The Overseer, upon reaching him they will transform into one of the roles below.

Roles

Formless Defender

The formless defender has a 60% chance of occurring. The defender is simply a shield around The Overseer. It will swarm around The Overseer to prevent non-piercing damage.

Formless Warrior

The formless warrior has a 30% chance of spawning, it will orbit The Overseer at ~8 tiles and will chase any player after they come within range. They fire out Formless Bolts which cause high damage if you are swarmed.

Formless Mage

The formless warrior has a 10% chance of spawning, it will orbit The Overseer at ~8 tiles and will run to and circle any player it comes near. Once it has a target it will begin shooting out sets of 3 Magic Discharges one at a time. These are the ones you want to focus down, their debilitating status effects won't kill you, but they will aid in your death.
